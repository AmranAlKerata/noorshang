About Noor Shang

Noor Shang is a leading global lifestyle, fashion, & leisure brand that was established by Norshang Cultural Gifts Co., Ltd., a Japanese Company. Our brand’s consumer centric philosophy is focused on enhancing quality of life by empowering our consumers with trendy functional aesthetic products designed to exude beauty and comfort. The Noor Shang brand has expanded into the Middle Eastern, European, and North American Markets through self-managed and franchise stores located around the world. Our passionate & dedicated management, design, & support team coupled with our valued franchisees have fueled our constant growth & expansion. Our belief in our team and our franchise partners has represented the foundations on which our foot print and customer base continue to multiply throughout the years. With a passionate focus on innovation, product research, & eco-friendly approaches, Noor Shang’s design team at the company headquarters have

developed proprietary, environmentally friendly, and sustainable products that enhance function, durability, & texture while reducing cost & eliminating waste. The market response from our innovative approach has been overwhelming and has helped us since inception to grow and transform into a global lifestyle brand with a complete line of innovative and desired eco-friendly products. As our footprint and growth expanded in the lifestyle industry, we set on to partner with innovative like-minded complimentary brands that are looking to create a positive impact on people and nature as well as bring out the beauty and aesthetics in our customer’s lives. In keeping with our new strategy, we partnered with Hili, a local Iraqi brand invented by a young Iraqi entrepreneur focused on empowering local artists and artisans to spread the authentic products of Iraq. We believe that such partnerships will help us spread our message faster and improve the standing of our global brand.

Hili’s Mission

Empowerment

We provide the opportunity for local women in our society to practice their expertise & skills to create designs passed down from generations with a modern trendy twist.

Awareness

Local Industry

We pride ourselves in using locally produced material & sourcing local talents to produce unique designs. We believe in the importance of supporting local products.

What We Offer Franchisees

Dedicated access to:

- Robust shipping, fulfilment, & transportation services that take the hassle out of product acquisition.

- The latest retail management supporting technologies through a unique franchisee single window ordering & inventory management software.

- A centralized operations center that provides comprehensive and timely services and support.

- An ever-growing number of designs, products, and models that are both high quality and extremely competitive and affordable giving our Franchisees a solid competitive advantage..

- No middle man framework, directly from our global lifestyle fashion brand manufacturing premises to your doors.

- Over 20 years of experience in understanding the retail needs and trends of foreign markets

- An online e-Commerce platform that can be setup in minutes to serve our new Franchisees with the backing and support of our marketing team

The Advantages of Our Franchisees

A vision and purpose behind the branding

The first circle represents circling back to the essence of what products should be of high-quality, organic, and affordable

The green in our logo symbolizes our commitment to sustainable, eco-friendly, and environmentally aware production and products

The lines represent our commitment to society and for being a positive force of giving back and strengthening the bonds with our community.

The second circle behind the main circle represents our ceaseless respect to our clients through the innovation and development of high-quality cost-effective products they love

The colors celebrate the joy of gifting, the beauty of memories entrenched in souvenirs, the soft feeling of walking on handmade carpets, and the thrill of enjoying authentic food.

Expansion of Our Foot Print
Expansion and growth remain a corner stone of our future’s vision. We plan to expand our reach to different markets and geographies through intensifying our Franchisee onboarding as well as empowering more and more like-minded affiliates to join our mission of influencing the world of lifestyle fashion.
Empowering Our Franchisees
We will continue to work hard on providing our Franchisees with more powerful and empowering tools and capabilities. Our plan is to improve our franchisee onboarding process, expand our digital offerings of support tools, scale the performance and functionalities of our single window platform that is responsible for order, inventory, billing, and shipping management, as well as launch an updated modern version of our eCommerce platform as a service to our franchisees.
Extension Of Product Lines
We aim to expand our product lines both vertically and horizontally, entering new product categories in the fashion industry as well as enhancing and augmenting our current product line offerings. As a leading lifestyle fashion brand, we must remain vigilant in ensuring that we continue to innovate, ideate, and reinvent designs, fashion, and products that meet the fashion trends of the market.
Digital Transformation & e-Commerce
In today’s world, no business will survive without investing in technology, digital transformation, and digital processes. That is why Nour Eight will always have a standing directive to continually devote effort, time, and money in strengthening our software solutions, platforms, and tools to meet our business needs evolve our business into a modern flagship technology driven enterprise. A core focus of this effort will be dedicated for developing a new modern e-Commerce customizable multi-tenant store front for our Franchisees that can be activated automatically on contract signature and onboarding.
